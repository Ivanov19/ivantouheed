Hello, Ivan here!

I am a final-year undergraduate student in Electrical & Electronic Engineering (EEE) at the University of Dhaka, Dhaka. My research interests lie at the intersection of materials science and artificial intelligence — specifically, leveraging machine learning to accelerate the discovery and design of nanomaterials for energy storage and electronic & photonic device applications.

I am currently working with Professor Dr. Imtiaz Ahmed, as one of the core members of our Materials Science Research Lab at University of Dhaka. I completed my higher secondary education at Notre Dame College, Dhaka — an institution I am proud to be a part of. Since then, I have worked on a range of embedded systems and hardware projects, including co-developing FabLab DU's first departmental drone — a low-cost ESP32-based quadcopter. I have also built ESP32-based handheld devices including a retro gaming console and a self-contained e-book reader with on-device Wi-Fi hosting, alongside earlier academic work in light-controlled sound systems.

My passions lie in reading and writing — Dostoevsky, Murakami, and Humayun Ahmed are permanent favourites. I also deeply enjoy cinema; I love watching, analysing, and obsessing over films, with Satyajit Ray and Christopher Nolan as my all-time favourite directors. And I love to eat — which, as you can imagine, makes weight loss quite the ongoing struggle. Feel free to check out my vlog if you'd like to read some of my writings!
