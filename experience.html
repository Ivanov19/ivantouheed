Research

Materials Science Research Laboratory (MSRL DU) Jan 2026 – Present

Undergraduate Researcher · Supervisor: Dr. Imtiaz Ahmed

ML-guided design of porous-carbon & metal-oxide electrodes for supercapacitors. I am building a structured literature dataset of porous-carbon electrodes and applying machine learning to map structural properties — BET surface area, pore-size distribution, and nitrogen/oxygen heteroatom content — to specific capacitance, screening for high-performance material configurations.

Model-shortlisted candidates are then validated experimentally across the full pipeline: precursor carbonization and chemical activation, structural characterization (BET, Raman, XPS), and electrochemical testing (CV, GCD, EIS) — closing the loop between prediction and physical behavior.
